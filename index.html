🎯 The Smart Hybrid Approach
At 4.25% your mortgage rate is below the long-term market average — mathematically, investing beats paying it down. But the best answer for most people is a hybrid:
Step 1 Max Roth IRA ($7,000/yr) + 401k to match — tax-advantaged investing first
Step 2 Build HYSA emergency fund to 3–6 months (~$15,000–$25,000)
Step 3 Split remaining cash flow: ~70% invest (brokerage/IRA) + ~30% extra mortgage
Step 4 As mortgage balance drops below $200k, consider accelerating payoff for the finish line
